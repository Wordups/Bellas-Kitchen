PRIVACY

★ THE SHORT VERSION ★

WHAT WE STORE

Your family name, your display name, and your meal orders. That's it. We don't ask for your email, your phone number, your address, or your birthday.

WHO SEES IT

Only the members of your family — meaning anyone holding your 4-digit family code. Bella's Kitchen does not sell, share, or analyze your data for any third party. Ever.

KIDS

The app is designed to be safe for kids. Display names can be anything (we recommend first name or nickname). No real names, no photos, no email, no in-app purchases, no ads.

YOUR DATA

Email hello@takeoff.llc at any time to export or delete everything tied to your family code.
